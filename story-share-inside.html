Sylvia Mulinge | Chief Customer Officer

“Notoriously Customer Obsessed asset is an always on internal engagement platform within the Customer Organization”

Notoriously Customer Obsessed asset is an always on internal engagement platform within the Customer Organizationthat aims to drive business and team engagement around our key objectives for the year. The platform adopts and amplifies various themes annually in line with our priorities and focus areas.

The Mission Ready edition will focus on providing updates on mission delivery, ensuring clarity and alignment across all teams and driving team engagement among the Customer Organization. This year’s engagement will focus on key business engagements, inspirational mission sessions in partnership with KDF, agile simulation and learning sessions as well as team engagement through experiential bootcamps in a competitive team format.
